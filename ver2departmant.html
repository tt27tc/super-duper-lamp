Vision

The College of Computing Studies envisions itself as a premier institution of higher learning that produces globally competitive professionals in the fields of computing, information technology, and data science. The College is committed to fostering innovation, ethical leadership, and community engagement through cutting-edge education, research, and sustainable practices.

Mission

The College of Computing Studies is dedicated to providing high-quality education in computing and information technology through dynamic and relevant academic programs. We strive to develop competent professionals equipped with practical skills, critical thinking, and ethical values. The College promotes research, innovation, and lifelong learning to address the evolving needs of industry, government, and society, contributing to the advancement of local and global communities.
